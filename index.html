About Eco-Farms & Foods

Eco-Farms & Foods is an agricultural startup. We do give knowledge about the need, methods, requirements of orgainic farming. As we all are aware of the soil pollution and poisonong of soil minerals due to the inorganic farming methods. So we at Eco-Farms & Foods train the people and make them learn about the organic farming. We also provide the farming equipments, organic fertilizers and manure. Special consultaion is also avialable at Eco-Farms & Foods.

Our Vision & Mission

Eco-Farms & Foods' vision is to contribute to the development of a sustainable food systems based on the organic philosophy and the organic principles. Eco-Farms & Foods contributes with new knowledge, which supports reliability, innovation and development of organic farming and food systems.We will provide an educational experience centered around a sustainable farming model which is healthy for the individual and the environment.​ We will ensure food accessibility through programs focused on putting the best healthy food possible into the hands of the community and those most in need. We will encourage a continuation of organic farming methods through training and providing the tools necessary for farm apprentices to move forward in their own pursuit of responsible land stewardship.

Our suggestion for your problems

It solves the problem of chemical fertilizers going into the groundwater, contaminating fish and frogs, and causing the soil to become void of nutrients and non-arable. The runoff from fertilizers causing algae in lakes and rivers. It poisons fish and aquatic plants. A switch to organic would increase biological activity in the soil (biodiversity) benefitting the soil with microbes and organic nutrients which enrich the soil, rather than destroy it like pesticides and fertilizers. Eventually we’ll have to feed an additional 300 million people so high tech will be used, only incorporating gm crops is a more likely option than fertilizers and pesticides
